About Me

I am a software engineer and I am a former airline pilot. Flying jet airplanes is a dream job. There is nothing like taking a 89,000 lbs airplane full of passengers up to 35,000 feet in the air and bringing it and all the passengers down safely again thousands of miles away. However, airlines are not very innovative. And rightly so they have to guarantee something works every time so they canâ€™t try anything new. I want to do my part to push the cutting edge.

I also have training as a leader. I have been a captain at my airline for about 5 years now. I am used to managing a team in stressful situations. I also have a depth of understanding when it comes to working with different types of people. As a captain I have to coordinate between other pilots, air traffic controllers, ground crew, flight attendants, and passengers. And the way I lead and interact with each group has to be different. I can bring this understanding of working with desperate types of people to the tech world.

I also love building things. I have taught myself how to paint and sew. I also build and fly radio controlled airplanes. I am able to bring these skills directly into software building. The thought process to designing a pattern for a sewing project or installing radio gear into a model airplane is the same as building software. I am learning the tools of software design and construction and I have found that my design skills help when it comes to building better software.

I am able to put together my desire to innovate with my skills as a designer and a leader into a very productive person. I am excited to roll up my sleeves and start working on some complex problems with a team.
